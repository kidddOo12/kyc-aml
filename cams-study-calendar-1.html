Exam Strategy & Tips

📋
CAMS format: 120 multiple-choice questions, 3.5 hours, computer-based, scenario-heavy (80% are scenario questions — not recall).
⚡
Time management: 1 minute 45 seconds per question maximum. If stuck, flag and move on. Return at the end.
🧠
Scenario approach: Read the question stem carefully. Identify the role of the person in the scenario (compliance officer? MLRO? analyst?). Then apply FATF standards to their situation.
🎯
Pass mark: ~75% (scaled scoring). Aim for 80% in all practice tests to give yourself buffer on exam day.
🇦🇪
UAE relevance: CAMS is the most recognised AML certification in DIFC and ADGM (Dubai/Abu Dhabi financial zones). FAB, Mashreq, Emirates NBD, and Western Union UAE actively advertise CAMS as preferred/required.
💡
Vikas advantage: You have real multi-jurisdiction SAR experience (APAC, EMEA, Americas), PEP/sanctions screening with CLEAR, and merchant risk investigations. The exam formalises what you already do. Your answers will be instinctively better than a fresh candidate.
