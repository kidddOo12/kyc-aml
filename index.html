What is KYC?

KYC stands for Know Your Customer, a process to verify the identity of clients.

What is AML?

AML stands for Anti-Money Laundering, regulations to prevent financial crimes.

Importance of KYC and AML

Both KYC and AML are crucial for financial institutions to prevent fraud and enhance security.
